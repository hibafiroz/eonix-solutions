> MongoDB Aggregation:

It is used to work with many documents and finally get one meaningful result. 
It works like a pipeline where documents goes step by step. Each step is called a stage.


> Core / Common Aggregation Stages in MongoDB:  starts with : {...}

1. $match
2. $project
3. $addFields
4. $group
5. $sort
6. $limit
7. $skip

> Other useful stages:

1. $lookup
2. $unwind
3. $count

Aggregation is mainly used for reports and analytics.
Example: Count users by city, Find total sales, Find average marks
Compared to find(), aggregation is more powerful because it can change and calculate data, not just fetch it.
